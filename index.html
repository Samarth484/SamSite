MHRD: Online Challenge SAMADHAAN

Ideation and Proof of Concept: The abstract of the our idea basically caters to the theme of Saving Lives. The idea is basically a fusion of a process and software technologies to facilitate its fruitful functionality.

The idea aims at helping the highly possible and prone second wave of this pandemic which may arise nationwide whilst uplifting the lockdown, during the later stages.
As can be deduced from the fact that the countries like China or Italy for that matter have experienced a second wave of Covid 19 being spread nationwide, there are high chances that after exiting the lockdown, (despite the fact that appropriate precautions would be taken then too) this invisible and highly contagious threat may spread amongst individuals owing to the fact that they’re under a controlled liberty of carrying out their life errands which would eventually result into social interaction.
Lets do a quick case study of China: the nation suffered to the core, went through the roof but later at the time when things stared getting neutral or better again and there was an appropriate alteration in the trend curve, China and other such affected nations exited the lockdown: although we can’t disguise the fact that there must have been appropriate measures to do so but then too owing to the fact that on an average 80% of the Covid 19 patients recover on their own without seeking any medical help, and there must be n number of cases of such patients who themselves don’t know that they have been infected by the virus or there must still be hundreds of non registered cases of virus infected people… Thus in such a case, these people who themselves don’t know that they have been infected or who aren’t registered on records to be an infected individual would surely in some way or the other indulge in social interaction at some point or the other( and this is something which can’t be taken under any other control except for self and whatsoever be the level of attention and precaution of various individuals, exiting lockdown poses danger of their social interaction in some way or the other for sure, maybe even unknowingly): now the infected individual is potent enough to transfer this virus to people in his or her proximity and one never knows the strength of the immune system of various people one is having around… thus this pinpointed example evenly reflects the fact that if this entire scenario occurs at different locations of the nation then there is a highly probable danger of a second wave which would exhaust up all the resources and economy to an adverse notch.

There’s an addon which we thought of:
The first and foremost thing is that the accounts being registered on the Arogya Setu Government app aren’t that appropriate because it’s a general trend that whenever a user sign up on the app, he or she has to face various interrogative counters regarding the current status of his or her health and travel history et cetera… Thus one gets cautious about the information one shares owing to the fact that one knows that this mandatory data to be shared is going to get registered on official govt. records, thus one checks in all the appropriate options which ensures to keep him flagged under the healthy or low risk section of being infected… and thus this results in forged or faulty data being uploaded on the servers, although we agree that there would surely be exceptions and people who register correct data as well but we cannot disagree the fact that the people falling in the first category is also gigantic in number. Thus keeping these views in mind we’ve come up with an idea and a solution prototype which can serve as our proof of concept regarding the concern in question:
Our idea is that a database should be devised which captures the health records of individuals working as an employee in any company or organization or any other recognized place for that matter, while entering the working premises such employees or workers have to pass through a quick health checkup update for example the body temperature etc. and this data would be stored then and there in real time on the server, and thus the cherry on the cake would be keeping an eye on the level of social interaction of people and their colleagues using the ML model which we have designed by getting in the data being fed to the model via the CCTV surveillance footages being fed in the model live and the model sends an alert if the distance between any two individuals is less than a threshold value and for more than a threshold amount of time, in addition to this there would be an eye on the frequency of coughing and sneezing of various individuals and all of this data which our ML model renders would be integrated in the database which is foresaid to be devised, and this thus using this data the level or percentage of health or the probability of any individual being affected could be figured out and an appropriate database with absolutely true data of the citizen... rest in video..
